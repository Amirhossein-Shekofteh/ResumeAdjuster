Data Analyst Intern — Northgate Retail Analytics

About the role:
Northgate Retail Analytics helps mid-size retailers understand their sales and
customer data. We're looking for a Data Analyst Intern to join our small analytics
team for a summer internship, supporting weekly reporting and ad-hoc analysis for
retail clients.

Responsibilities:
- Write SQL queries against our retail sales data warehouse to pull data for
  recurring and ad-hoc reports.
- Clean and analyze data using Python (pandas) or Excel.
- Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau or Power BI to track sales, inventory,
  and customer trends.
- Summarize findings in clear, non-technical language for client-facing analysts.
- Assist senior analysts with statistical analysis (e.g., trend analysis, basic
  forecasting) for quarterly business reviews.
- Participate in team stand-ups and document your analysis process.

Requirements:
- Currently pursuing a degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science,
  Business Analytics, or a related field.
- Working knowledge of SQL (able to write joins, aggregations, and filters).
- Familiarity with Python or a similar language for data manipulation.
- Experience building dashboards or visualizations in a BI tool (Tableau, Power BI,
  or similar) is strongly preferred.
- Comfortable working with Excel for smaller analyses (pivot tables, formulas).
- Strong written communication skills — able to explain data findings to
  non-technical stakeholders.
- Coursework or project experience with statistics (hypothesis testing,
  regression, or similar) is a plus.

Nice to have:
- Experience with version control (Git).
- Prior internship or part-time work involving data reporting.
- Familiarity with retail or e-commerce data.
